Reach 1 Million Deployments

In approximately 10 years or less

 Posted on November 28, 2021  |  4 minutes  |  Archie Cowan

When will your team achieve 1M deployments in their environments? We do mean 10^6 deployments. 1M deployments may be difficult to believe if your team practices a monthly deployment or a quarterly deployment to your production environment. A team deploying to production every month will reach 10^6 deployments in a little over 83,333 years. A team deploying to production quarterly needs 250,000 years to reach 1M deployments.
